Hyderabad's Must-Try Food
Hyderabadi Biryani

A world-famous dish made with aromatic basmati rice, flavorful spices, and tender meat.

Irani Chai

A creamy and strong tea best enjoyed with Osmania biscuits in Hyderabad's famous cafes.

Haleem

A Ramadan-special delicacy, Haleem is a slow-cooked stew made with wheat, lentils, and meat.

Double Ka Meetha

A sweet delicacy made from fried bread soaked in sugar syrup and flavored with cardamom.
